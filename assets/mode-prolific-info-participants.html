Information for Research Participants

Thank you for agreeing to participate in this study. Please read the information below before proceeding.

📌 Research Project: Human and AI-Generated Review Classification

This study explores how well people can differentiate between AI-generated and human-written reviews.

🧑‍🏫 Conducted by:

Researchers at the Business School, University of Nottingham.

🔎 What will participants do?

You will classify 50 short product reviews and indicate your confidence. The task takes ~10 minutes, and you will receive **£5 compensation** via Prolific.

🔐 Data Privacy

Your responses are anonymous and securely stored. The results will be analyzed in aggregate.

📢 Research Outcomes

Findings may be used in academic publications and conferences.
